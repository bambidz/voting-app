Welcome to Learning Lab!

Our aim is to provide a better learning environment so that learners at any age can enjoy learning and prosper in their fields of interest.

We are esperically interested in the social aspect of learning where leaners learn from and with others. Being a social animan, human learns from others at her/his early stage of life and learns to learn with others as soon as she/he gets along with peers and becomes a natural social learner.

We are also interested in all scales of learning whether they be spatial or temporal. Learning can be defined in many different ways depending on various perspectives.

A change in behavior that occurs as a result of experience. A slow modification of the connectivity between neurons. A change in long-term memory. Inferring plausible models to explain observed data.

Our methods encompass both quantitative and qualitative. We use machine learning approach to discern hidden patterns of human behaviors and cognitive and psychological approach to analyze discourse among humans.
